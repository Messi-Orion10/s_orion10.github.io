ACC on a self-balancing wheeled robot (GRANDLAND)

Components:

* Arduino Pro Micro
* 2 pcs. NEMA 14 stepper motors
* 2 pcs. model car wheels
* 2 pcs. A4988 Stepper motor driver modules
* MPU-6050 gyro/accelerometer breakout board
* 3 cell LiPo battery
* 20 pcs. M3 16 mm
